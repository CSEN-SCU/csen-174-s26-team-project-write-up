Write Up

A writing mentor in the margins—explains why a word lands flat, suggests sharper options, and never silently rewrites you.

What it is

A browser extension plus a small local API. You send a passage; it opens a floating panel with vocabulary feedback, synonyms, and teaching notes. Save synonyms into your thesaurus (grouped by This piece is…) or star words for later.

Who it’s for

Aspiring writers and students who want a roadmap of linguistic blind spots—not just instant fixes—aligned with the Write Up vision (personalized mentor + diagnostic mindset).

Problem it solves

Tools like Grammarly optimize the “now.” Write Up explains why diction weakens your clarity and voice, and nudges you toward habits you can carry across drafts.

Try it in four steps

  1. Open Goals (top right) once—set your level and what you want feedback to emphasize.
  2. Optionally describe this piece (email, lab report, informal essay…) so suggestions match tone and register.
  3. Highlight text (or Ctrl+C / Cmd+C), then Analyze selection or Analyze clipboard. Or right-click → Analyze with Write Up.
  4. Read the floating panel on the page—use ＋ to save a synonym (filed under your current This piece is… line).

Demo laptop: the API server must be running (npm start in server) and Groq configured, or the panel will be empty. Use Settings for the API URL.
