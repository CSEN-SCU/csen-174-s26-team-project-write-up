Get a writing analysis in a timed setting.

This prototype simulates a short, timed writing moment—so you can build fluency under pressure and receive real-time feedback.

What this app does

On the next page you get a random prompt. After you press Ready a text area appears and a 15-minute countdown starts. Use Submit when you are finished, or stay until the timer ends—either way you are taken to a third page with pattern-style feedback plus a LanguageTool grammar and spelling check (your draft is sent to LanguageTool’s servers for that step—see the note on the feedback page).

The idea aligns with learning and mastery over instant correction: no pasted “perfect answer,” no homogenized tone—just space to write, revise in your own words, and receive suggestive feedback. This prototype in particular focuses on feedback and practice associated with interview or timed writing scenarios, and is meant to help users prepare for those situations.
